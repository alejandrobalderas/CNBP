Welcome to CNBP

CNBP is a company with a rich history of quality manufacturing. Over the years and decades, we have adapted and diversified right up to the present day, where we now produce world class instrumentation equipment, primarily for use in the oil industry.

Our clients and ever changing industry drive our future with the majority of our products now designed and built uniquely to individual client specifications. Our portfolio is increasingly spreading across mainland Europe.

CNBP has always supported local employment and continues to do so, with two sites based in the UK in Leeds and Glasgow. We encourage highly motivated students from the local area to embark upon a technical career, working with highly skilled technicians and engineers under the caring and watchful supervision of Production Director, C.J. Bateman.

Based in the UK, we also build products for clients spread across Europe.

There is nothing standard about CNBP.

Awards

Oil Instrumentation Industry Quality (OIIQ) Award

CNBP fought off stiff competition to be crowned OIIQ winner. The award was presented at a special gala event, attended by all the major players in the oil industry. This is considered the top award for this industry.

Unfortunately, CNBP's vastly experienced Production Director, C.J. Bateman, could not be there, having a prior engagement with the local horticultural society. Accepting the award was Bobbi Marriot, UK Managing Director, who offered, "This is recognition of CNBP's never-ending quest to produce high quality products for our clients, championed by our C.J."
