Locations

Leeds location is our main site and head office. The larger of the two sites, as well as being a manufacturing site, we also run our HR, Sales and Marketing and Administration from this location. Leeds is traditionally an industrial city, which has always been able to supply highly skilled and technical resources for our business. Its central UK location gives it excellent transport links, with major road and rail networks being on the doorstep. The port of Hull is only one hour away, which provides rapid links to mainland Europe.

Based in the industrial Scottish power house, Glasgow is Scotland's largest city with a rich history of manufacturing and a hardworking population. The Glasgow site is purely for manufacturing and building the whole range of products. Like Leeds, it has fantastic access to a highly skilled population with diverse experience and knowledge. This enables CNBP to build products, which our clients demand. As so much of the UK oil industry is based around Scottish waters, our strategic Glasgow site enables to build and supply products quickly.
